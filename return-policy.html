Return Policy

We want you to love your LUXE MODAL pieces

30-Day Return Window

We offer a 30-day return period from the date of delivery for unworn items in original condition with all tags attached.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions:

  • Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Unworn and unwashed
  • In original condition with all tags attached
  • In original packaging (if applicable)
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase (order confirmation email)

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Gift cards
  • Items that show signs of wear or damage
  • Items without original tags
  • Undergarments and intimate apparel (for hygiene reasons)

How to Return

Step 1: Contact Us

Email us at returns@luxemodal.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll send you a return authorization number and instructions.

Step 2: Pack Your Items

Pack your items securely in their original packaging (if possible). Include the return authorization number and a copy of your order confirmation.

Step 3: Ship Your Return

Ship your return to the address provided in your return authorization email. We recommend using a trackable shipping service.

Return Shipping Costs

  • Customer-Initiated Returns: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs
  • Defective/Incorrect Items: We cover return shipping costs
  • Exchanges: Free return shipping for exchanges

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return:

  • Approved returns will be refunded within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method
  • For COD orders, refunds will be issued via bank transfer or store credit
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable (except for defective items)

Exchanges

We're happy to exchange items for a different size or color:

  • Follow the same return process and indicate you want an exchange
  • Exchanges are subject to availability
  • We'll ship your exchange item free of charge once we receive your return
  • If the new item costs more, you'll be charged the difference
  • If it costs less, we'll refund the difference

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us immediately at support@luxemodal.com
  • Include photos of the damage/defect
  • We'll provide a prepaid return label
  • You'll receive a full refund or replacement
  • We'll also refund original shipping costs

Quality Guarantee

All LUXE MODAL products come with a 1-year quality warranty against manufacturing defects. This warranty does not cover normal wear and tear, misuse, or improper care. To make a warranty claim, contact us with your order number and photos of the issue.

Store Credit

Returns can be processed as store credit instead of refund:

  • Receive an additional 10% bonus when choosing store credit
  • Store credit never expires
  • Can be used on any future purchase

International Returns

Currently, we only ship within the United States. International returns are not applicable at this time.
