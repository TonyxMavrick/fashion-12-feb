Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in original condition with tags attached. Visit our Return Policy page for details.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently we ship within the United States only. We're working on expanding international shipping soon.

Are your products really sustainable?

Yes! All our products are made from GOTS-certified organic or GRS-certified recycled materials, produced in Fair Trade facilities. Learn more on our About page.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Expedited options available at checkout. Free shipping on orders over $150.
